About-Us
eCourierz.com is a L - SaaS (Logistics Software as a Solution) platform for Multi-Carrier Shipping that connects Individual eCommerce Sellers, SMEs & Enterprises with the best Courier Service providers through integrations to their Sales Channels. We have built a comprehensive set of Carrier Library comprising of various Domestic and International carriers. eCourierz began its journey in 2015 with a mission to offer an One-stop convenient logistics solution to any individual or enterprise for their express parcels or documents and today the company has firmly entrenched itself as the 'Knowledge Leader' and 'Market Leader' of eCommerce & logistics. eCourierz offers a wide range of innovative logistics services like Imports, Exports, Shipping solution to amazon FBA, International and Domestic shipping, Bulk orders and many more. The firm adds maximum value to businesses at every level, right from offering multiple courier solutions, providing easy online courier booking support to ensuring delivery of goods anywhere in India. Currently, the company provide services to 12000+ pincodes, COD services to 7000+ pin codes, free pickup from 100 cities in India, and services to more than 5000 eCommerce sellers. The company has also tied ups with all the world class courier partners including FedEx, DHL, TNT, Aramex, Dotzot, Delhivery, UPS, DHL eCommerce, Gati, Bombino Express, Antron and many more to handle the Domestic as well as International Shipments. Our idea is to mainly focus on sellers who sell on different marketplaces and offer them cutting-edge logistics solutions, enabling them to pay particular attention on their core competencies. Any Regular Shipper / Seller who sells on various marketplaces like Amazon, Flipkart, eBay, Etsy etc are welcome to open an account with us to get an easy & full access to our features. They will get major benefits through our platform including discounted rates, cashback offers and so on. Our vision is to set standards for Global Shipping through newest/innovative technology, making it convenient for everyone to use.
